An Ontology of Gelato

Based on a scientific Article:"A review on Gelato: An Italian Delicacy", this ontology describes the basic elements of Gelato: ingredients, nutrients, flavours, stabilizers, alternative sweeteners, health benefit are few of the components of a recipe that had origin in the 16th century in Italy. Today Gelato is still one of the most popular frozen desserts in the world.
Let's explore the reason why!

Ontology

The Project

The making of gelatOntology

With our top to down ontology we would like to represent the knowledge collected in the selected article which include several scientific studies and an overview about the historical origins and diffusion of these frozen food in the various countries of the world, comparing the two basically different kind of dessert.

The process of making gelatontology consists of:

  • Coming up with competency questions in natural language
  • Scour the article for answers to the competency questions. Break the answers into classes, object properties, data properties and individual
  • Arrange classes, object properties, data properties into an ontology
  • Run Sparql queries to check the competency of the ontology

Competency questions

The competency questions were first written in natural language to make inquiries into the article about gelato.

What are the key nutrients in gelato?

What are the basic ingredients used in the making of Gelato?

What are the ingredients that are used additionally to flavor gelato?

What are the common types of stabilizers often used for gelato?

What are some common alternative sweeteners used for gelato?

How much total fat does gelato contain generally?

What are some of the popular gelato flavors around the world?

What are the health benefits of gelato?

What is the percentage of solid non-fat of gelato?

What are the preparation steps for gelato?
